Tue, Sep 01 - 6:30PM
How to Cofound a Startup!

What does it mean to be a cofounder, and how you can be a good one?  A panel of three cofounders will share their experiences and knowledge. Topics include how and when to start up a company, how to find product/market fit, and when to be a full time founder.

Learn from 3 of Houston's best cofounders! 

Panelists are:

Sameer Soleja is the founder and CEO of Molecule Software (molecule.io), a Houston-based startup that makes the best energy software on Earth.  Prior to Molecule, Sameer was a consultant at SunGard for 10 years, and taught energy IT at the University of Houston.

John Phan is the co-founder of Qwiqq (qwiqq.me), a Tech Wildcatters-backed mobile commerce startup. He has been involved at the founder or executive level at five tech startups and has helped build and launch new product categories in online marketplaces, enterprise software, and digital communications.

Jordan Thaeler is co-founder and CEO of WhatsBusy (whatsbusy.com), a San Francisco startup that publicly displays when places are busy by ingesting point of sale data. Jordan is an engineer by training and advises startups in strategy, M&A, financing, and operations as he's served his corporate overlords in that capacity prior to founding WhatsBusy.
